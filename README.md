# ⚙️ DiaGear

An Android application designed to help individuals with type 1 diabetes track the availability of medical equipment covered by the Croatian Health Insurance Institute. DiaGear simplifies the process of finding and monitoring diabetes management gear on the official list of approved additional medical equipment.

## ✨ Features

- **Equipment Tracking** - Monitor availability of approved medical devices
- **Nearby Pharmacies** - Locate nearby pharmacies through an integrated pharmacy map that provides an insight into pharmacies located in your area
- **Real-time Updates** - Stay informed about device availability changes
- **User-Friendly Interface** - Simple and intuitive navigation for easy access
- **Offline Support** - Access cached equipment information when needed

## 📋 About the Project

DiaGear addresses a practical need for individuals managing type 1 diabetes in Croatia. The Croatian Health Insurance Institute maintains a list of approved additional medical equipment that patients can access, but they are published in spreadsheet form which is often hard to read through and find relevant information. This application streamlines the process by providing an easy-to-use interface to browse and track these resources.

The app focuses on improving accessibility and awareness of available diabetes management tools, helping users make informed decisions about their healthcare equipment.
